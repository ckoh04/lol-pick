About This Tool

This League of Legends team maker helps custom game lobbies build fair 5v5 teams. It compares player ranks and role preferences to create balanced matchups quickly.

The tool is designed for in-house games, community nights, and scrim-style setups where players want a fair split without manual calculations.

How to Use

  • Add up to 10 players with rank plus primary and secondary roles.
  • Click Balance Teams for optimized matchmaking.
  • Click Random Teams if you want a less structured result.
  • Review team averages and role assignments before starting your game.

FAQ

How does team balancing work?

The app compares rank points, prioritizes role fit, and searches for the best split across both teams.

Do I need an account to use this?

No account is required. You can open the page and start adding players immediately.

Does this tool store player data permanently?

No. Player entries are kept in browser memory for the current session and reset on refresh.

Privacy Policy

This site does not require login and does not store personal data on a backend server. Information entered into the team maker is processed in your browser session.

We may use third-party services such as Google AdSense to show ads. These services may use cookies or similar technologies to personalize ads and measure performance.

You can manage ad personalization and cookie settings through your Google account and browser privacy controls.
